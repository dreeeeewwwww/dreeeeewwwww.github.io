Mapping Capital Bikeshare rides from my nearest station

Mapbox GL
Hexgrid Heatmap
Capital Bikeshare Q1 2017 data

I was curious to see where Capital Bikeshare riders in my neighborhood were heading when checking out bikes from the station nearest to my house (1st and O St NW, ID 31519). The results are mostly what you'd expect, with rides generally terminating in busy parts of town (downtown, U Street, Dupont Circle, Columbia Heights).

Only a few riders went far afield, with a small cluster of trips ending on Kennedy St NW, and one single rider who made it to the Iwo Jima memorial in a blazingly fast 31 minutes. Though this is only data from a single station, it still illustrates how Anacostia remains on the edge of the system. Zero rides from my nearest station ended in that part of the city.

          mapboxgl.accessToken = 'token-foo';

              style: 'mapbox://styles/mapbox/dark-v9',
              center: [-77.012164, 38.908569],
              zoom: 12

          map.on("load", function() {
              var heatmap = new HexgridHeatmap(map, "hexgrid-heatmap", "waterway-label");
              heatmap.setIntensity(15);
              heatmap.setSpread(0.05);
              heatmap.setData(bikeshare);
              heatmap.update();
              heatmap.setColorStops([

          });
